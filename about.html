About Stellar Academy

A place where tradition meets innovation, and students are empowered to dream, create, and achieve.

Our Story

Founded in 1998, Stellar Academy began as a small community school with just 60 students. Today, we are proud to be one of the leading independent schools in the region, offering a rigorous academic program alongside diverse opportunities in arts, athletics, and leadership.

Our philosophy is simple: education should ignite curiosity, build resilience, and cultivate character. We blend tradition with modern pedagogy to create a learning environment that is both challenging and supportive.

Our Mission & Values

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in academics, arts, and athletics, empowering students to achieve their personal best.

Innovation

Our programs encourage creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving through experiential and project-based learning.

Community

We foster a culture of kindness, respect, and responsibility where every student feels valued and supported.
